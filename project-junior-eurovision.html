Creating broadcast graphics for one of Europe's biggest televised music events, watched by millions.

Broadcast Design Team

Overview

For Junior Eurovision, I worked on broadcast graphics and visual assets for a live international show. Working under hard deadlines with zero room for on-air mistakes, the team delivered a consistent visual language across screens, stage, and promo materials.

Featured Image

The Challenge

Live television forgives nothing. Every lower third, transition, and full-screen graphic had to work the first time — across different broadcast systems, languages, and last-minute changes to the running order. We believed in clarity under pressure: a design system simple enough to adapt in minutes, strong enough to feel premium on air.

The Challenge Visual

The System

I helped build a modular graphics package — templates for every recurring on-air moment, from contestant introductions to voting sequences. This structure let the team react to live changes without breaking visual consistency.

System Visual

The Result

The show aired live to an international audience with a seamless on-screen experience. The workflow and templates we built became the starting point for the channel's next large-scale productions.
